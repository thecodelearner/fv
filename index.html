Future Value of a series of monthly payments

A simple 'C' programming implementation to estimate future income from interest for a series of monthly payments

Task:

Suppose a person decides to save a fixed amount of money at the end of every month for 'n' years. If the money earns interest at 'i' percent per annum, calculate how much money will be accumulated after 'n' years based on interest rate and the frequency with which the interest 'i' is compounded (Annually / Semi-annually / Monthly / Quarterly).

Where:
